    About

    remberX is an idea that have evolved over years. How to use the computer to mitigate my limited memory. Over time working with Semantic Web technologies the idea expanded and also using the remberX as a personal profiler, i.e. be better to know who I am than Google. This page is intended to investigate interest, I use remberX as a project for the course "Startup Engineering" at the University of Stanford in Coursera.
